Most agent-engineering debates on the timeline never get past the screenshot stage:
someone claims context engineering beats RAG, someone else says tool sprawl is the new
microservices sprawl, everyone quote-tweets, nobody ships a measurement.

This blog is the opposite. Each post takes one live argument from the agent-eng
conversation, turns it into a probing engineering question, and answers it by building the
smallest possible thing — usually a [Genkit](https://genkit.dev) app — and reporting what
actually happened. Real code, real numbers, a takeaway you can use.
