Most agent-engineering debates on the timeline never get past the screenshot stage. Someone claims context engineering beats RAG. Someone else says tool sprawl is the new microservices sprawl. Everyone quote-tweets. Nobody ships a measurement.

This blog is the opposite.

Each post takes one live argument, turns it into a probing engineering question, and answers it by building the smallest possible thing — usually a Genkit app — and reporting what actually happened. Real code, real numbers, a takeaway you can use.
